# Partner project: [name]

One-page record of a free-to-mission-partner, partner-backed continuation project. Copy this file, rename it to a short kebab-case slug (e.g., `docs/engagements/volunteer-scheduler.md`), and fill it in. Keep it current as the project progresses.

## Problem

What specific problem is being addressed, in the partner's own terms where possible. Not "they need an app" — the actual pain: what breaks today, what it costs.

## Mission partner

Who the mission partner is, and who the domain steward is. See [`docs/people/roles.md`](../people/roles.md).

## Redemptive angle

Who is worse off today, what specifically will be different for them, and why this team can be trusted to finish and sustain it. See [`docs/vision/redemptive-frame.md`](../vision/redemptive-frame.md).

## Scope

What's in the minimum durable scope for this project, and what's explicitly out of scope for now.

## Team

Lead, practitioners, and their roles. See [`docs/people/roles.md`](../people/roles.md).

## Backing

What volunteer time, funding, tools, mentorship, professional services, or other resources make this project possible without charging the mission partner. Name the backing partners and any agreed constraints.

## Status

One of: `discovery` / `scoped` / `active` / `paused` / `complete` / `declined`. Include the date of the last status change.

## Outcomes

Filled in as the project progresses and at close: what was delivered, what the handoff looked like, what the mission partner's experience was, and what the team learned that should feed back into the model (link a decision record in [`docs/decisions/`](../decisions/) if it changes the model).
